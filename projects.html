Projects

Ducks Lawn Care https://duckslawncare.pythonanywhere.com/
  • Built a full-stack web application for a small business to receive, manage and track enquiries for contract work.
  • The Contact Us form triggers an email to the business owner via a Flask route. It is sent with Flask-Mail using Gmail as the email client and includes the enquiries details.
  • APScheduler is used to send a daily email summarising how many enquiries were received for the day, and how urgent they are, providing a helpful summary for the business owner.
  • CSS Bootstrap is used for mobile optmisation. On a mobile browser, the site’s modules will collapse into a single column, ensuring optimal visibility and user experience.
  • The Staff view has an integrated pagination system. Enquiries can be marked as ‘Contacted ’ which uses JavaScript to update the status on the front-end and trigger a Flask route to update the status on the database.
  • Enquiries with a ‘Not Contacted ’ status show their age in ‘Days and Hours’, to give the business owner a clear idea how long a customer has been waiting.
  • Auto Dark Mode is supported and triggers based on device settings.
